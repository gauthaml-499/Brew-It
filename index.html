Our Story

A neighbourhood coffee shop, done properly.

Brew-It started with a simple idea: Kirkby in Ashfield deserved a coffee shop that took the coffee as seriously as the welcome. Every bag is chosen for flavour, every cup is pulled to order, and every bake is made to be shared.

Drop in for a flat white on the way to work, linger over a pot of filter with a friend, or grab a toastie and a seat by the window. However you take your coffee, you're always welcome at 40 Kingsway.
